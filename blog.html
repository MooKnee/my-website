EQ in an IQ World

Emotional Intelligence (EQ) is the ability to understand, manage, and effectively express one's own emotions, as well as engage and navigate successfully with those of others. [As defined by Psychology Today]

  • Check your ego, but not your intuition, at the door. – No one sysprog has all of the answers, and we know this. The mainframe is a field that will teach you something new every day of your career. You can choose to learn kicking and screaming - or you can say "Thank You" and take a lap around the office for a breather. Every mainframer's goal is ensuring production stability, and you will experience some who get a little too passionate in expressing why they think their solution is the most safe and efficient. Remember that you are all working towards the same goal, achieveing all the nines of availability, but if something doesn't sit right with you, do speak up!
  • Make FriendsYour network is your net worth. Helping others in the office outside of your specific role's capabilities is a great way to make friends, expand your connections, and grow your presence. This is how you get to thrive, not just survive, in a corporate setting.
  • Give back - mentor others – Mentor both people who were in your shoes and those who weren't. Reflect on their experiences, and your own, to assist them in steering their ship.
  • When in doubt, be kind. – You never know what someone is going through.
